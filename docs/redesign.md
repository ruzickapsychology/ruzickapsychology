# Current Visual Direction

This document captures the live brand direction. For implementation rules, use `docs/design-system.md`.

## Brand Feel

Ruzicka Psychology should feel warm, grounded, tactile, and quietly polished. The visual language borrows from paper, stationery, vintage portraiture, soft florals, and clinical calm without becoming ornate.

## Core Ingredients

- Warm paper background with subtle grain.
- Deep aubergine ink for headings and primary actions.
- Cream and warm feature bands for section rhythm.
- Dusty rose accents for links, eyebrows, icons, and soft interactive states.
- Real photography only for Dr. Ruzicka and the office.
- Floral imagery where it supports warmth and calm.
- Thin, translucent dividers for structured grids.
- Gentle motion with reduced-motion support.

## Typography

- Libre Baskerville carries headings and body copy.
- Manrope supports eyebrows and compact sans labels.
- IBM Plex Mono supports utility labels, nav, buttons, and metadata.

The site intentionally uses a small type system:

- Hero and page headings.
- Section/module/item headings.
- Body 1, Body 2, Body 3.
- Eyebrow/meta/utility labels.

## Layout

The site should stay aligned to the shared `Container` and `site-grid` system. Sections should use `Section` sizing instead of one-off padding. Full-bleed image sections may break the background visually, but their text and inner containers should remain grid-aligned.

## Imagery

On-brand:

- Soft focus florals.
- Warm paper or textile textures.
- Real office interiors/exteriors.
- Real practitioner photography.
- Images with gentle contrast and enough calm negative space for type.

Off-brand:

- AI-generated portraits or likenesses.
- Generic stock therapy imagery.
- Harsh medical imagery.
- Dark, moody images that obscure content.
- Decorative images that slow the site without supporting trust or clarity.

## Ethical Note

The site avoids client testimonials by default because this is a licensed healthcare practice. Do not add testimonials without explicit legal or ethical review.
