---
title: Finding Your Footing After Change
date: 2025-12-15
excerpt: A reflection on transitions, steadiness, and making room for support.
---

Major life transitions can ask more of us than we expected. Even welcome changes can unsettle familiar rhythms, relationships, and the way we understand ourselves.

Therapy can offer a steady place to notice what is shifting, name what feels tender, and build practical support around the season you are in.
